Package Details

Web Design

Begins with a 1-hour consult to go over your current website or websites you admire to discuss design and functionality that you're looking for. After the meeting we'll set up a sales strategy session and incorporate those goals to the final design of your site.

  • Custom site - Me building a template or theme from scratch to meet your unique needs.

*Website design takes 4-10 weeks depending on complexity

Business Goals Review

Your website needs to be helping you achieve your business goals. You will fill out a short business goals worksheet and we'll meet for a 30-min review session to brainstorm the ways your website can help you reach those goals. These will be used alongside your design preferences to create the BEST website for you.

Sales Strategy & Marketing Review

Your site must work relentlessly on bringing in sales.
After you've filled out your business goals worksheet, we will meet for a 1-hour sales strategy and marketing session.

Content Review

All content on your current or future site will be reviewed for usability and SEO.

Usability - I'll be asking:

  • -What is the main purpose for your page?
    Does this align with your business goals?
  • -How easy is it to understand the main call to action?
  • -Is there anything too busy or potentially confusing to visitors?
  • -Uniformity? Does everything look like it belongs here?
  • -Can Google & other search engines easily understand and rank this page?

SEO (Search Engine Optimization)

Alongside the content review is work done for SEO. The review covers any content problem areas that must be addressed for SEO purposes. The SEO planning will be done in the development stage using plugins, tracking codes, and SEO best practices to make your website ready to rank.

Support

I won't leave you in the dark when you're done working with me. You'll be learning along the way and I'll be available for questions during and after the process. I'm available up to 2 weeks after, and also have retainer options for extended periods of time.
